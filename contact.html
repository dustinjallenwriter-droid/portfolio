I'm a technical writer with a background in philosophy, education, and the arts. My writing career has taken me from scripting gold rush theatre and penning grant proposals for historic sites, to teaching students how to understand and write about obscure 18th century German philosophy, to developing UX copy in digital marketplaces. The common thread? Clear, engaging communication that resonates with a diverse range of audiences.

I specialize in technical documentation, educational content, editing and proofreading, UX writing, and content strategy. I bring both rigor and creativity to my work, whether I’m building structured help systems or writing copy for a digital audience.

Outside of work, I pursue creative projects of many kinds: touring with my folk band, performing comedy, writing cultural analyses, volunteering in community education initiatives. I believe storytelling in all its forms contributes to a better world.

You can explore my portfolio for samples, view my résumé, or get in touch.
