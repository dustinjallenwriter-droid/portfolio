About Me

I'm a technical writer with a background in philosophy, freelancing, and the arts. My writing career has taken me from scripting gold rush theatre and penning grant proposals for historic sites, to teaching obscure ancient philosophy, to copywriting for digital marketplaces.
The common thread? Clear, engaging communication that resonates with a wide range of audiences.

I specialize in technical documentation, educational content, editing and proofreading, UX writing, and content strategy. I bring both a rigorous eye for detail and a sense of creativity to my work.

I also pursue creative projects of many kinds: touring with my folk band, performing comedy, analyzing culture on my blog, or volunteering for a range of humanitarian projects.
