About Me

My background in philosophy, freelancing, and the arts has helped me refine my technical communication skills. As my portfolio shows, my writing career has taken me from scripting gold rush theatre and penning grant proposals for historic sites, to teaching obscure ancient philosophy, to copywriting for digital marketplaces.

The common thread? Clear, engaging communication that resonates with a wide range of audiences.

My eye for detail and sense of creativity help me create effective technical documentation, educational content, UX writing, and content strategies. I also have a strong interest in editing and proofreading.

Outside of work, I pursue creative projects of many kinds: touring with my folk band (not as frequently as when we were young), acting and performing comedy, analyzing culture on my blog, and volunteering for a range of humanitarian and community-based projects. Currently, I'm an Adult Literacy Volunteer at West Neighbourhood House.

Explore my portfolio for samples, view my CV, or get in touch.

I’m deep in the thick of writing and designing a literary cookbook. It’s based on the works of the Japanese metaphysical fiction writer Haruki Murakami, featuring some of the dishes that appear in his works. Spaghetti Murakami coming soon.
